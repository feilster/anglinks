## Maps

### Generate map for video
Use Map Puzzle to generate hi-res map:
1. choose Base (Google Maps - Map)
2. enter lat and longitude (get by clicking on specific spot on Google Maps)
3. set Image Settings - 
* Zoom - 11
* Width - 4000 
* Height - 2560
4. and Download
